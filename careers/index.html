Join our team

A place to build a career. We genuinely value our staff.

Here at Concert, we get to know everyone and are as committed to building relationships with the people that form our organisation internally, as we are to those outside of it.

It’s also important to us that we build a culture of success. Our steadfast dedication to supporting our employees in their career development helps them to fulfil their aspirations for growth – as well as helping us achieve ours.

We do all of this while promoting equality, diversity and fairness in the workplace.

We are always seeking the best talent to join our team. If you would like to learn more about opportunities with us at all levels then please email us at careers@weareconcert.com

What makes a Concert person?

Building relationships since 1974.

Friendly and inquisitive. Concise and collaborative. Ambitious and determined. These are just some of the qualities that make a Concert person.

We’re considerate, respectful, reliable and proven. We can unravel complex problems and are experts at understanding – and meeting – our clients’ needs.

Most importantly, we pride ourselves on our honest relationships, built on the trust and mutual respect we’ve been developing collectively as a company for more than 40 years.
