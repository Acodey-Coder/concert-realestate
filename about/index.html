Who we are

Bringing Harmony to Every Project.

Here at Concert, we invest our time and energy to fully understand our Clients real estate objectives and use our expertise and knowledge to bring projects to life.

The complexities of the building process can often be a challenging environment and we believe our philosophy and collaborative approach will deliver successful projects for all those involved.

Our team of highly skilled professionals have a range of skills and experience and approach every new project with an enthusiasm to deliver value which exceeds expectations.

Concert is an independently owned business and is able to trace its history back to 1974, when Bruce Shaw Partnership was founded and we are proud of the relationships we have built with clients and colleagues during the past five decades.

We continue to support our Clients to deliver industry leading projects in the UK and Europe through the provision of project management, cost management and health & safety consultancy.

As a company, we embrace change. As part of our name change and re-brand, we have engaged with our clients and thought carefully about how we present ourselves to reflect our mission for strong leadership, with a collaborative approach and harmony on every project. Our new brand capitalises on everything we have learned from the last forty-seven years in the industry, to better communicate our offering to the construction sector.

We were very pleased with the experience and performance of the Concert team. Their professionalism and expertise gave us confidence and we look forward to working with Concert again in the UK and other regions.

Jack Ke, Data Centre Manager, Europe, China Mobile International

Concert and the wider community

We outline the importance of Concert values for both our staff and the wider environment

Workplace
Concert believe collaboration between staff are at the forefront of productivity and wellbeing. Our people are our most valuable assets and we are committed to nurturing and investing in their talent and skill sets.

Community
Working with the local community, our staff have raised funds for an array of charities through the many endeavours of our dedicated social and community team from cake bakes and charity days to city running and Green and Clean Days.

Charity
Our current nominated charity is The Construction Youth Trust whose aim is to inspire and enable young people to overcome barriers and discover a career in the construction and built environment sector. We hold regular and annual themed days to bring the Concert team together to raise monies for them, for more information see their website: www.constructionyouth.org.uk

Environment
Concert implement and maintain an effective environmental management system which our staff are familiar with. They are encouraged to identify any areas of improvement so we can continuously seek to enhance our output and raise company standards.
